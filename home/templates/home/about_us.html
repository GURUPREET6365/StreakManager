About Streak Manager

Our Mission

Streak Manager helps you build and maintain positive habits through simple daily tracking. We believe consistency is the key to personal growth, and our goal is to make habit tracking effortless and motivating.

What We Offer
  • Simple Tracking: Mark daily tasks complete with one click
  • Progress Visualization: See your current and longest streaks at a glance
  • Goal Management: Set and track daily goals alongside your habits
  • Privacy First: Your data belongs to you and is kept secure
Who We Are

Streak Manager was built by a student developer passionate about productivity and habit formation. This project started as a personal tool to track coding habits and grew into a full-featured habit tracker designed to help anyone build consistency in their life.

Our Values
  • Simplicity: Clean, intuitive interface without unnecessary complexity
  • Privacy: We collect only what's necessary and never sell your data
  • Transparency: Open about how we operate and what we do with your information
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular updates based on user feedback

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Streak Manager collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • First and last name
  • Password (stored securely using encryption)

Usage Data:

  • Streak titles and descriptions you create
  • Daily goal entries
  • Completion dates and streak statistics
  • Account creation and last update timestamps
How We Use Your Information
  • Provide Services: To create and maintain your account, store your streaks and goals
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Communication: To send password reset emails and important service updates
  • Improve Service: To understand how users interact with features and make improvements
Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Passwords are hashed using Django's secure password hashing (PBKDF2)
  • Data is stored in a protected database with access controls
  • Connections use HTTPS encryption in production
  • We do not store payment information (service is currently free)
Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Hosting Provider: To host and serve the application
  • Email Service: To send password reset and notification emails

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Edit: Update your account information at any time
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (feature coming soon)
Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal information and associated streaks/goals are permanently removed from our systems.

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics at this time.
