Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QuickPrep collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address and phone number (optional)
  • Username and full name
  • Password (encrypted and hashed securely)

Learning Data:

  • Your study notes and learning materials
  • Practice questions and your answers
  • Revision history and learning statistics
  • Last login and activity timestamps
  • Study preferences and goals
How We Use Your Information
  • Provide Services: Store and manage your notes, generate practice questions, schedule revisions, and track progress
  • AI Enhancement: Analyze your learning patterns to improve AI accuracy and generate better study materials for you
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and keep your account secure from unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send you password resets, security alerts, and important service updates
  • Service Improvement: Understand how you use QuickPrep to improve features and user experience
Data Storage and Security
  • All passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption (PBKDF2 with Django)
  • Your notes are stored in a secure database with role-based access controls
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption in production environment
  • We perform regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • We do not store payment information (QuickPrep is completely free)
  • Data is backed up regularly to prevent accidental loss
Third-Party Services
  • Hosting: We use secure cloud hosting providers to store and serve your data
  • Email: We use email services to send you important notifications
  • AI Services: We may use AI APIs to generate practice questions (your data remains private)
Your Rights
  • Access: Download all your data anytime from your account settings
  • Edit: Update your profile information and delete specific notes
  • Delete: Request complete account deletion with all associated data permanently removed
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop our use of your data for improvements and analytics
Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal information and study materials are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking
  • We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that track your behavior
  • Your privacy preferences are always respected
Children's Privacy

QuickPrep is intended for students aged 13 and above. If you are under 13, please obtain parental consent. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13 without consent, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the website. Your continued use of QuickPrep after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
